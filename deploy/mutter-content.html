LONG STORY SHORT,

brand identity / ux-ui / publication design

project overview —

“long story short,” is a podcast for young adults who are going through the emotional process of growing up. “long story short, 2021” is an established issue of this podcast, where we discuss the change on how a young adult approaches their adulthood, and the realization: how they are growing up in this unprecedented situation, how their surroundings have changed, and the way they are coping with that change.

goals —

This project aims to evoke the feelings of uncertainty in its audience, while providing a sense of camaraderie: growing up is hard, but you are not in this alone. With the deliverables and the narrative presenting itself throughout, “long story short,” wants to engage its audience’s 3 senses: sight, hearing, and touch, to get them immerse in the emotional experience.
