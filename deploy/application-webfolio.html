RESPONSIVE WEBSITE DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT

responsive web design / front-end development / creative direction

overview ‚Äî

During my tenure as the Lead Web Designer and Graphic Designer at MOST Consulting Group, I have worked on 10+ end-to-end responsive website design projects. This webfolio showcases my process of developing these websites from ideation to launch, and highlights the diverse range of projects I have worked on for clients in various industries.

goals ‚Äî

Each client has a different goal in mind for their website projects. From brochure to e-commerce websites, our team work closely with clients to develop a strategy that we follow through every step of these projects. We aim to create user-friendly and accessible web products based on user research, while also keeping our clients' marketing interests in mind.

client research and strategic planning ‚Äî

We begin the first stage of any website design with a series of strategy meetings with our clients to brainstorm ideas and help us decipher their expectations for the website and its functionality. While we take the lead on all aspects of the process, we do like to take a collaborative approach to ensure that the finished site contains all of the necessary features and fits the client's overall brand vision.

project outline sketches & figma wireframes ‚Äî

After working with our client's team to understand the goals and direction of envisioned for a new website project, we started by creating a website outline and wireframe of the website's various pages, sections, call-to-actions, and features. I develop various pencil sketches, including multiple options for each page of the website, using the website outline our team create from strategic planning meetings. These sketches then get thoroughly reviewed within our team of designers, and I take these feedbacks into account to create low-fidelity Figma wireframes based on my sketches.

These wireframes offer a range of layout options for each page of our website project. By utilizing different layouts and features, I can effectively design low to high fidelity wireframes, from mobile to desktop as well. I refer to our strategic planning interviews with clients, as well as relying on user research to ensure intuitive and easy UX for our clients' target audience. A majority of our clients' web projects require more complex interactions, so usually a desktop-first design approach is a more relevant option. However, all of our web projects are responsive across all media queries, so we always develop mobile-optimized wireframes as well.

design with branding ‚Äî

After getting feedbacks from our clients on the layout and overall usability of the website, we move on to the design phase where we incorporate various brand elements, identify appropriate typography and imagery, and set the tone for the overall look and feel of the website. A draft of the homepage is the first piece we present to the client, usually after two weeks, to get their feedback on the direction we're heading. After a meeting to review progress and some adjustments, we have a framework that we can apply across the website's e-commerce pages and other sections.

Website design is a detailed, collaborative process that requires thorough planning and clear communication to get it right. Working closely with Willie's Remedy, we ensured that we met all requests from key stakeholders and that the website contained all the features they needed, like a loyalty program, custom iconography, and assets for retail partners.
