ACROSS: WORD PUZZLE KIT

brand identity / package design / ui design

project overview —

Across: Word Puzzle Kit is a puzzles box targeting adults. Across was designed with the intention of keeping the audience entertained within every inch of the box. Each piece of copywriting or graphic points them to a new mystery. The kit contains a guidebook, pencils and eraser, puzzle cards, and a binder.

goals —

By leaving Easter eggs around the box, the design allows the audience to explore the kit at their own pace to find the hidden compartment while still being able to enjoy the puzzles. Across aims to create a calming yet intriguing environment for its audience and take the frustration out of the process of solving a puzzle.
